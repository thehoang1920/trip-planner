## Day 1 — Dec 26 (Sat) — Arrival

**Flight:** SGN 12:15 → SIN 15:20

### Airport exploration

Singapore Airlines usually arrives at **T2 or T3**. From arrivals, follow signs to **Jewel Changi** (connected via link bridge, 5 min walk).

| Activity | Where | Time | Cost |
|----------|-------|------|------|
| HSBC Rain Vortex | Jewel L1 (world's tallest indoor waterfall) | ~20 min | Free |
| Shiseido Forest Valley | Jewel L1–L4 (indoor forest walking trails) | ~20 min | Free |
| Canopy Park | Jewel L5 (sky nets, mazes, Discovery Slides) | ~30 min | S$8/pax |
| Butterfly Garden | T3 Transit L2 (1,000+ live butterflies) | ~15 min | Free |
| The Slide@T3 | T3 Public L1 (12m, 4-storey slide) | ~5 min | Free (Changi Rewards) |
| Sunflower Garden | T2 rooftop (runway views) | ~10 min | Free |
| Cactus Garden | T1 rooftop (outdoor desert garden) | ~10 min | Free |
| Free Movie Theatre | T2/T3 transit (24h, latest films) | pick one | Free |

- [ ] 15:20 — Arrive Changi Airport (T2/T3)
- [ ] 15:45 — Clear immigration, head to Jewel
- [ ] 16:00 — Explore Rain Vortex + Forest Valley
- [ ] 16:30 — Optional: Butterfly Garden (T3) or Canopy Park (Jewel L5)
- [ ] 17:00 — Move to hotel, check in
- [ ] Evening: explore Geylang area (food, walk around)
